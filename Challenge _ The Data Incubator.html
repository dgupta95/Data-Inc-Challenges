Section 2: A circular road has NN positions labeled 00 through N−1N−1 where adjacent positions are connected to each other and position N−1N−1 is connected to 00. MM cars start at position 00 through M−1M−1 (inclusive). A car can make a valid move by moving forward one position (or goes from N-1 to 0) if the position it is moving into is empty. At each turn, only consider cars that have a valid move available and make one of the valid moves that you choose randomly with equal probability. After TT rounds, we compute the average (AA) and standard deviation (SS) of the position of the cars.
